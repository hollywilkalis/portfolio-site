About me

I coded my first website using Microsoft Notepad, Netscape Navigator (remember that one?), and a copy of HTML for Dummies. It included frames, and probably some "under construction" graphics from time to time. Those were the days.

But I've improved since then, and these days I'm enjoying my deep dive into the mysterious world of frameworks, package managers, and missing semi-colons.

When I'm not coding, I enjoy reading, photography, gardening, writing ridiculous social media posts, volunteering, and being outdoors. I like to hike and kayak, and I love being at the beach. I'm also a cymbalist with the Last Regiment of Syncopated Drummers and the volunteer coordinator for the Portland Old Time Music Gathering.
