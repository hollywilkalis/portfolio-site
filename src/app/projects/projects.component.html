Oregon Health Authority site migration and update

I had the chance to spend about eight months working with my agencys web team to migrate the site to a new version of Sharepoint with responsive layouts. We faced a lot of challenges, including outdated content, thousands of broken links and orphaned documents, and the need to accommodate a wide variety of editor skill levels. But I'm proud of the work I did to create a site that is more usable for both staff and the public.

Bootstrap, HTML, Sharepoint
